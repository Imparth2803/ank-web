Step 1 — What type of ledger?
This determines how Debit and Credit columns are interpreted in the ANK calculation.
📤
SALE
You are the seller.
Debit = customer owes you.
Credit = customer paid back.
📥
PURCHASE
You are the buyer.
Debit = you paid supplier.
Credit = supplier owes you back.
